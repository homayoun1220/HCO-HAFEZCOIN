Highlights

• Scarcity alone is insufficient: resource structure determines influence cost.
• Parallelizable resources admit C(s,T)=o(sT) regardless of protocol design.
• Throughput-bounded resources enforce C(s,T)=Omega(sT): a sharp separation.
• Resource Substitution Principle: linear cost requires breaking parallelizability.
• Calibration on Ethereum PoS and Bitcoin PoW confirms the gap is quantitative.
